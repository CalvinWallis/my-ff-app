About me

Beginning my professional career as a Full-Stack developer allowed me to dip my toes in many different disciplines across computer science. Maybe it is a little vain, but I found that my favorite work was the stuff that I knew users would touch and be able to appreciate.

For the last couple of years I have turned my focus entirely to the front-end world and strive to become an expert in the field.
